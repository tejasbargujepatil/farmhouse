Our Story

A Home Away
From Home

Nestled in the lush Konkan heartland, Gangutara Nivas is where time slows down and nature takes over. Set amidst sprawling mango orchards, coconut palms, and the gentle whisper of a forest, our farmhouse offers an authentic Maharashtra countryside experience unlike any other.

Whether you seek a peaceful weekend retreat, a family holiday away from city noise, or simply a place to breathe — Gangutara Nivas welcomes you with warm Konkan hospitality and open arms.

Located in the serene Gaonale, Dapoli, every sunrise paints the sky gold, every evening brings birdsong, rustling leaves, and the cool coastal breeze of the Sahyadri foothills.

The Experience

Moments That Stay With You

01

Morning Nature Walks

Begin your day with a refreshing walk through coconut groves, mango orchards, and paddy fields. Breathe pure Konkan air, listen to birdsong, and feel completely at peace with the world around you.

02

Hammock Leisure Time

Spend golden afternoons gently swaying in the hammock under tall coconut palms. Let the cool breeze carry away all your worries. This is what true relaxation feels like — unhurried, uninterrupted, and completely yours.

03

Family Gatherings

Create cherished memories with your family in our spacious farm setting. From festive celebrations to quiet retreats, Gangutara Nivas provides the perfect backdrop for your most precious moments together.

04

Authentic Village Life

Immerse yourself in the warmth of Konkan village life at Tale. Wake up to roosters crowing, savour authentic Maharashtrian flavours, and reconnect with the simplicity and beauty of rural India.

Find Us

In the Heart of
Konkan Beauty

Gangutara Nivas is ideally situated in the scenic village of Tale, Dapoli — one of Maharashtra's most beloved nature destinations. Surrounded by the lush Sahyadri ranges, pristine coastal air, and emerald green forests, our location is itself a destination.

Dapoli is known for its stunning beaches, eco-tourism, ancient temples, and breathtaking hill views — making it the perfect base for exploration or pure relaxation.
